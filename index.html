GCETTB Service and Facilities

Courses

We are known not only for the breadth of choices, but for the normal choices for prospective and well-known programmes. The college provides a rich academic experience that encourages hands-on learning in every programme.

Programmes

For about a century, we have a creed of creativity in the way to carve out a niche for ourselves. We have an environment to hear ideas, cultivate curiosity, explore people's passions, and develop habits of lifelong learning that enrich life and understanding.

Library

The college library offers a wide range of science and technological books and journals. The students can borrow two books at a time for a maximum period of four weeks. Besides they are also entitled to get one book from the book bank.

Faculty

The college provides the best teaching facilities to all the students. Smart Classes and Digital Learning methods are also incorporated in day to day teaching curriculum

Graduation

Prospective students are our most important clientele, and our service and the manner in which we deliver it reflect that primacy. To the greatest degree possible, our new comers are given personalized and supportive attention.

Placements

Our Training and Placement activities offer an opportunity for interaction of students with industry. The college has very strong links with the industry and continues to receive generous support that proves to be effective in paving the path for a bright future. We also encourage participation in various IIT/IISc certified certificate courses for the students.
